DevOps is a modern software development methodology that combines Development and Operations to streamline the software delivery lifecycle. It emphasizes collaboration, automation, and continuous improvement to deliver high-quality software faster and more reliably.

Key Principles and Goals

DevOps focuses on breaking down silos between development and operations teams, fostering a culture of collaboration. Its primary goals include faster software releases, automation of repetitive tasks, continuous integration and delivery (CI/CD), and robust monitoring to ensure system reliability.
